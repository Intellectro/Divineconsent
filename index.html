About Us
Our Story
Divine Consent International was registered in Nigeria on the 17th day of June, 2013, with Corporate Affairs Commission, under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 1990 part B to undertake transport services, real estate, oil and gas, building materials, construction and agro business. The company is an indigenous outfit and 100% owned by Nigerians.
Divine Consent International is a Quick growing entity and a dynamic company that engages in general contacts, general merchandise and trading of goods and services. The company is well known distributors with Dangote Cement Company, Dangote Sugar Company and BUA Sugar Company with expansive market share of the cement business in North Central, North East, South East and North West. The company is well focused in achieving the goals and objectives of its stakeholders. We have purposeful and experienced leadership and staffs that are positioned to provide top level satisfaction to her customers.
